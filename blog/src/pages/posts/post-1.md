# First Flight: A Historic Opening Weekend for the Valkyries

The wait is finally over. This past weekend, the Golden State Valkyries officially took flight, marking a new era for women's basketball in the Bay Area. The energy was electric, the "Valkyrie Violet" was everywhere, and the basketball lived up to the hype as the team secured a perfect **2-0 start**.

## Friday, May 8: The Inaugural Statement (at Seattle)

The Valkyries made a loud statement on the road, defeating the **Seattle Storm 91-80** at Climate Pledge Arena. The team showcased incredible range, hitting **15 three-pointers** to silence the home crowd.

## Sunday, May 10: Home Sweet Home (vs. Phoenix)

Team,1st,2nd,3rd,4th,Final
Phoenix Mercury,24,11,22,22,79
GS Valkyries,22,31,18,24,95

History was made at Chase Center as the Valkyries dominated the **Phoenix Mercury 95-79**. A massive **31-11 second-quarter surge** effectively sealed the victory early, proving that the home-court advantage is very real in San Francisco.

### Key Stats:
* **Final Score:** Valkyries 95, Mercury 79
* **Janelle Salaün:** 21 points on 7-of-10 shooting
* **Gabby Williams:** 19 points
* **Kayla Thornton:** 19 points
* **Paint Presence:** Outscored Phoenix 38-24 in the paint
